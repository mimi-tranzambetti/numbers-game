A Numbers Game is a two-player game that plunges you and your friend, side-by-side, into the intimidating labyrinth of modern-day romance -- all through cards! Draw cards to form unique characters, roll dice to reveal more about your potential lovers, develop genuine bonds, and stumble through rejection and dejection as the two of you lonely, lonely millennials search for your soulmates. If you feel lost, don’t worry. Remember - dating is just A Numbers Game. Are you in?
