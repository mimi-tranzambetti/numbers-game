What is A Numbers Game?


A Numbers Game is a competitive card based dating sim where you and another person play as petty, image obsessed young adults who want a more socially desirable date than the other person so they can feel superior.

When you’re an image obsessed young adult who wants a better date than the other person, dating is just A Numbers Game. Play against your friend in a competitive card based dating sim - Are you in?




Gameplay


Each potential date is composed of 5 cards: their name (“profile”), occupation, hobby, personality, and quirk.

With the exception of profile, each card has a number indicating that trait’s social value. The higher the value, the more socially desirable, and the more likely you are to have found the arm-candy you’ve been looking for.

Players can spend up to 3 action points per turn: actions consist of:

  • Looking for a date
  • Going on a date with a character to learn more about their traits
  • Dumping a date
  • Stealing a date from the other player
  • Becoming “exclusive” with a date

Each action has a different amount of action points based on the gravity or work needed for each scenario, which drives players to strategize and plan their next moves.

The game ends when either both players are exclusive with an S.O. or the profile deck is exhausted. Whoever has the S.O. with the greatest social value score is the winner!
